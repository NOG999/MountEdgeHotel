Welcome

A Tranquil Escape in Sri Lanka's Hill Country

Nestled in the heart of Nuwara Eliya, Mount Edge Hotel offers a serene haven for travelers seeking the beauty of Sri Lanka's central highlands. Surrounded by lush tea plantations, misty mountains, and cool refreshing air.

Whether you're a couple on a romantic getaway, a family on holiday, or a solo traveler seeking peace, we welcome you with comfortable rooms, friendly service, and an unforgettable atmosphere.
