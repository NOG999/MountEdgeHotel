Our Story

A Family Tradition of Hospitality

Mount Edge Hotel is a charming, family-run hotel nestled in the beautiful hills of Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka — often called "Little England" for its cool climate and colonial heritage. Located at No.73, Dharmapalapura, Blackpool, our hotel offers a warm and welcoming escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Surrounded by lush tea plantations, misty mountains, and vibrant gardens, Mount Edge Hotel was established with a simple vision: to provide clean, comfortable, and affordable accommodation for travelers who wish to experience the natural beauty and serenity of Sri Lanka's hill country.

Over the years, we have welcomed guests from across the globe — solo travelers, honeymooners, families, and adventure seekers — all drawn by the promise of fresh mountain air, stunning scenery, and genuine Sri Lankan hospitality.

The Destination

About Nuwara Eliya

Nuwara Eliya, perched at an altitude of 1,868 metres above sea level, is the heart of Sri Lanka's tea country. Known as "Little England," this charming hill station features a cool temperate climate, colonial-era architecture, and some of the most spectacular scenery in South Asia.

The region is famous for its world-renowned Ceylon tea estates, stunning waterfalls, pristine lakes, and botanical gardens. Whether you're trekking through Horton Plains, boating on Gregory Lake, or enjoying a cup of fresh Ceylon tea amidst the plantations, Nuwara Eliya offers experiences that stay with you forever.

The best time to visit is from March to May and August to September, though the cool climate makes it a year-round destination.
