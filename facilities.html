Our Facilities

Amenities

Everything You Need

Every facility at Mount Edge Hotel is designed to enhance your comfort and connection with the beautiful nature surrounding us.

📶

Free Wi-Fi

Complimentary high-speed internet access throughout all areas of the hotel.

🛁

Attached Bathroom

Every room features a private en-suite bathroom with consistent hot water supply.

🍴

In-House Dining

Delicious Sri Lankan and international cuisine with flexible Room Only, Half Board, and Full Board plans.

🚗

Free Parking

Secure on-site parking available for all guests at absolutely no extra charge.

🌳

Garden & Outdoor Area

A lush garden and outdoor seating area with panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

👤

24/7 Reception

Our friendly team is available round the clock to assist with any requests.

🚌

Airport Transfers

We can arrange comfortable transfers from Bandaranaike International Airport upon request.

🗺️

Tour Arrangements

Our staff can help plan and arrange local tours including tea estate visits, Horton Plains treks, and more.

🧺

Laundry Service

Laundry and ironing service available for guests at reasonable rates.

☕

Tea & Coffee

Complimentary fresh Ceylon tea and coffee available in all rooms throughout your stay.

🛏️

Extra Beds

Extra beds available on request for families, subject to availability and room type.

📦

Luggage Storage

Secure luggage storage available for early arrivals and late departures.

Dining

Authentic Cuisine

Our kitchen takes pride in serving freshly prepared meals using locally sourced ingredients from the hill country. From traditional Sri Lankan rice and curry to continental breakfasts and hearty dinners, every meal is cooked with care.

We offer three flexible meal plans: Room Only, Half Board, and Full Board, so you can choose exactly what suits your travel style.
